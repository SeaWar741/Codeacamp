About Juan Carlos Garfias

"It's the execution that matters, never the idea"

Casey Neistat

I’m a 17yr old guy who loves computers and graphic design. I’ve been working with PC’s since I was a child. I’ve always felt passion towards learning new things and decided to learn and understand more than the average people do about computers. I began coding in 2017 at Codeacamp with Ruby on Rails. Currently I'm starting with Java and C++.

Right now, I’m in high school at Tecnológico de Monterrey in the International Baccalaureate program. I’m looking forward to study Computer science or Computer engineering.

I’ve participated in several student groups such as Taman Keet 3933 where I worked in the programming department (Robotics Representative Team of Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Santa Fe), Activatec (Events Organization Group at of Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Santa Fe) as the group president and in the Audiovisual Production group of Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Santa Fe.

Regarding graphic design; I’ve worked with the adobe suite since 2007. I have experience in all the programs, working in projects with companies such as JJ Eventos Especiales. I’ve also taken diplomas in 3D modeling at Escena learning Zbrush and Maya.

I have experience with video editing and filming thanks to my participation in short film productions with groups such as La Matatena. A.C where the films “Mini historias de animales con humanos” and “Pavex quiere ir a la universidad” were sent to film festivals through the world.

My main interests are Web, Software and Graphic Design, Video Production and Cyber Security.

My mother tongue is Spanish. I’ve been studying English since I was a baby, right now I have the B2 level and I’m taking the advanced English program of the International Baccalaureate. I also speak French and currently have the A2 level. I’m starting to learn mandarin Chinese and hope to take a certification soon.
